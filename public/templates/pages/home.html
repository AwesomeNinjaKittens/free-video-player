The free HTML5 video player

Welcome to the Free Video Player project! This is a HTML5 based open source free video player that has the goal to provide the possibility of playing both regular video content aswell as adaptive streaming technologies such as DASH and HLS. What it is? This is an Open Source Video Project following the GPLv3 license. Wanna know more?

  • Download for free
  • Contribute as a developer
  • Open Source - GPLv3
  • HTML5 Technology - Adaptive Streams
  • Plays DASH/MP4/MOV/AND REGULAR WEB CONTENT
  • Handles subtitles for VOD (Video On Demand) content
  • Video Controls API
